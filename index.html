At Meikles Foundation ,we are dedicated to coordinating and facilitating corporate social responsibility initiatives that bring about meaningful and sustainable change. With a focus on education, healthcare, environmental conservation ,community development, and poverty alleviation , we strive to make a difference in the lives of as many people as possible in Zimbabwe
